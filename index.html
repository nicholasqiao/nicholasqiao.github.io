I have a passion for all things programming and development. I'm constantly jotting down ideas for new projects. I have an insatiable drive to explore and learn new things, and I'm always ready to share what I know with those around me. Clean code and clean design are what I strive to achieve.

Other Interests

Besides development, my interests are far and wide. I have a deep-seated interest in internet security. I grew up envisioning myself as an elite hacker with 20 monitors surrounding me as my fingers danced across the keyboard like a pianist would play the piano. Although I might not become an elite hacker, I'll settle for the 20 monitors.

As with any geek at heart, I enjoy a healthy dose of video games. But more than playing, making games is where the real fun lies. I love the ability to express my creativity and ideas through this type outlet.

Just recently, I've returned to the ancient game of Go. I find it's simplistic rules, yet almost infinite complexity both intriguing and incredibly fun to play and learn. I love the aspect of self-improvement and self-growth which the game embodies.
